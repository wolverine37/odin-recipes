Pork giniling with basil leaves with basmati rice

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp peanut oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 chillies, finely chopped
  • 500g pork mince
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 cup basil leaves
  • 2 tsp sweet soy sauce
  • steamed rice and 4 fried eggs, to serve

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok over high heat.
  2. Add garlic, onion and chillies, and cook for 30 seconds or until fragrant.
